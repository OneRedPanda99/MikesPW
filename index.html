Risks of DIY Power Washing

While it may be tempting to try to power wash your property yourself, there are significant risks associated with DIY power washing.

01
Property Damage

Without the proper equipment, knowledge, and experience, DIY power washing can damage your property. This includes damage to siding, windows, doors, and other exterior surfaces.

02
Personal Injury

Power washing involves working with high-pressure water sprays that can be dangerous if improperly handled. DIY power washing can lead to personal injury due to falls, slips, or other accidents.

03
Environmental Damage

Improper disposal of cleaning solutions used in power washing can lead to environmental damage. Mikes knows how to properly handle and dispose of cleaning solutions to ensure we do not harm the environment.
